01 — The Manifesto

Why we refuse to be like every other gym.

We don't sell mirrors. We sell progress.

No air-conditioning is the point. Real gyms run hot. Big industrial fans, open-air walls, the kind of training environment that forges actual athletes — not lobby selfies.

เราไม่ใช่ยิมที่ขายแอร์ — เราขายผลลัพธ์จริง

[ 01 ]

Open-Air Built

Reviewers keep saying it: "no AC, but cooler than I expected." High ceilings, cross-ventilation, industrial fans. You came here to sweat — we made the room for it.

[ 02 ]

Hyrox Specialists

Sled push lanes, ski-erg, wall balls, sandbag stations. Coached programming for the world's fastest-growing fitness race — at every level.

[ 03 ]

Hospitality, Thai-Style

Every member who walked through the door this year mentioned the team. Service mind from the front desk to the platform. Nobody trains alone here.

[ 04 ]

Fuel On-Site

Cold drinks, real food, whey, recovery snacks — all in-house. Park free, train hard, refuel without leaving the building.
